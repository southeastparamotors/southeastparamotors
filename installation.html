Installation Guide

Follow these steps to install your Paramotor LED Light Kit.

  1. Unpack the kit and ensure all components are included.
  2. Secure the LED strip around the hoop using the provided zip ties.
  3. Connect the wiring harness to the control box.
  4. Attach the battery and test the lights.
  5. Ensure everything is secured before your flight.

If you have any issues, contact us at support@southeastparamotors.com
