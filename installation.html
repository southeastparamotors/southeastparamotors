Installation Instructions

Follow these steps to install your Paramotor LED Kit:

  • Step 1: Secure the LED strip along the paramotor frame.
  • Step 2: Connect the wiring harness to the control box.
  • Step 3: Attach the battery pack securely.
  • Step 4: Turn on the system and test LED functionality.
